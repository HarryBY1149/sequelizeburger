About Yes

Yes is an English progressive rock band formed in London, 1968 by Jon Anderson, Chris Squire, Peter Banks, Tony Kaye, and Bill Bufford.

Originally performing a mix of new music and arrangments of pop and rock songs, the 70's saw the band shift to progressive rock, where they found commercial success across multiple albums.
The album Fragile and its hit single Roundabout were recorded during this era.

Yes disbanded after a decade of touring in 1981, but reformed with a new lineup and a more commercial musical direction in 1983. They have continued touring with varying lineups ever since, excepting a brief hiatus in the 90's.
